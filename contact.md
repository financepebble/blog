## How to Activate the Contact Form

To make this contact form work, you need to set up a free Formspree account:

1. Go to [Formspree.io](https://formspree.io) (free - 50 submissions/month)
2. Create a new form
3. Copy your form ID (looks like `xpznabcd`)
4. Replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` in the form action above with your actual ID

**Alternative:** If you prefer, you can simply list your email address here instead of using a form.
