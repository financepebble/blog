# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## What We Collect

**Automatically collected:**
- IP address, browser type, pages viewed, time on site
- Cookies for analytics and affiliate tracking

**You provide:**
- Name and email when you contact us

## How We Use It

- Improve website content and user experience
- Respond to your inquiries
- Analyze traffic to create better content

## Third-Party Services

We use services that collect data:

- **Google Analytics** - Traffic analysis ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Microsoft Clarity** - User behavior analysis ([Privacy Policy](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement))
- **Cloudflare** - Security and performance ([Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/))
- **Affiliate Networks** - Track referrals via cookies
- **GitHub Pages** - Hosting ([Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement))

Each service has its own privacy policy.

We use cookies for analytics and affiliate tracking. You can disable them in your browser settings, but some features may not work.
